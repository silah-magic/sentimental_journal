# MindTrack 
A privacy-focused daily journal that uses Natural Language Processing (NLP) to help users track their emotional wellbeing over time.

## The Problem
Many people struggle to identify patterns in their mental health or realize when they are entering a period of high stress.

## The Solution
MindTrack provides a simple space to write. As you save entries, the app analyzes the sentiment of your writing and visualizes your "Mood Trend" over weeks and months.
